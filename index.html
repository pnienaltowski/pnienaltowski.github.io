I am currently a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions ESR Fellow at PHYMOT and a doctoral student at the ETH Zurich, Department of Civil, Environmental and Geomatic Engineering. My research explores microbial motility in collaboration with Lyncee Tec and the Stocker's group at the Institute of Environmental Engineering, with a particular focus on 4D tracking of microbial dynamics using holographic imaging.

Before joining ETH Zurich in 2021, I earned my Master’s in Mechatronics and Photonics Engineering at the Warsaw University of Technology, Faculty of Mechatronics. During my undergraduate years, I engaged in several research projects with the BioPhase Imaging group, supervised by Małgorzata Kujawińska.
