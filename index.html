I am currently a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions ESR Fellow at PHYMOT and a doctoral student at the ETH Department of Civil, Environmental and Geomatic Engineering. My research focuses on microbial motility, collaborating with Lyncee Tec and the Stocker's group at the Institute of Environmental Engineering. I have a particular interest in the 4D tracking of microbial dynamics using holographic imaging.

Before joining ETH Zurich in 2021, I completed my Master's at WUT Faculty of Mechatronics, where I majored in mechatronics and photonics engineering. During my undergraduate years, I engaged in several research projects with the BioPhase Imaging group, while supervised by Małgorzata Kujawińska.
