I am a doctoral student and MSC Early Stage Researcher at the Lyncee Tec and ETH Department of Civil, Environmental and Geomatic Engineering . I study microbial motility with Roman Stocker's group at the Institute of Environmental Engineering , with a special interest in 3D tracking and digital holography microscopy.

Prior to starting at ETH in 2021, I was an undergraduate at WUT Department of Mechatronics, where I majored in mechatronics and photonics engineering. During this time, I also worked on a number of projects in BioPhase Imaging group while supervised by Malgorzata Kujawinska.
