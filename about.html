Patryk Nienaltowski

I am a Research Engineer at Lyncée Tec SA, where I develop image processing pipelines and analysis algorithms for 4D data acquired with holographic imaging. My work focuses on extracting quantitative information from complex biological and material systems.

I received my Ph.D. from ETH Zurich, where I specialized in holographic imaging and the quantitative analysis of microbial motility as part of the Stocker Lab at the Institute of Environmental Engineering.

Before joining ETH Zurich in 2021, I earned a Master's degree in Mechatronics and Photonics Engineering from the Warsaw University of Technology. During my studies, I participated in research projects within the BioPhase Imaging group under the supervision of Małgorzata Kujawińska.
